About Me

Taymor Abou-Zaki is a remarkable individual with a unique journey that blends culinary expertise and a passion for technology. For 16 years, Taymor's culinary journey unfolded as a dedicated chef, skillfully crafting gastronomic delights that delighted palates and left lasting impressions. With a culinary career that spanned over a decade and a half, Taymor honed not only the art of cooking but also the valuable skills of creativity, precision, and teamwork.

Transitioning from the culinary world to the realm of technology, Taymor embarked on a new chapter of growth and learning. As a student at Central Washington University (CWU), Taymor has embraced the challenges and opportunities presented by the world of information technology. With a focused pursuit of excellence, Taymor is on the verge of achieving a Bachelor of Arts degree in IT Administration with a specialization in project management.
